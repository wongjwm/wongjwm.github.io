NEU's Chinese Student Association

Northeastern University's Chinese Student Association is a student organization that supports awareness of Chinese and Chinese American culture and strives to unite students of all backgrounds interested in Chinese American heritage through general meetings and larger events.

As the sole designer for the Chinese Student Association on campus this past academic year, I was tasked with all things involving media for the club, including designing posters, social media marketing material, club cards, club apparel, etc., as well as taking photos and videos during events. Here is some of the work I produced during my time as designer for the club!

Posters created for a select few of the club's larger, annual events which featured up to 250 guests!
A crewneck design inspired by koi fish, which represent good luck and fortune in Chinese culture.
